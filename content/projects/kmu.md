Ongoing project.

From the project page:
> The aim of the project is to investigate how new approaches and techniques, such as virtual and augmented reality (VR and AR), can be used for innovative education and training concepts in companies to alleviate these difficulties.

Specifically, a system will be developed that will enable remote training using video streaming through an assembly of hardware and software components, while enhancing the learning experience of remote participants through innovative interaction capabilities and deployed AR and VR techniques.

In parallel, the system will be tested on developed training scenarios and evaluated during the project.
