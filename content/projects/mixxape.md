A cassette deck for the web: drop in a list of YouTube videos and mixxape plays them back as a shareable mixtape — no ads, no algorithm, no history.

<!--more-->

![the friendly mixxape](../../img/mixxape-1.png#small)

mixxape wraps the YouTube player in a tape-deck interface and packs the whole playlist into the link itself, so a mixtape is just a URL you can send to a friend. It's a small antidote to algorithmic feeds — you pick the tracks, hit play, and listen without the distractions.

More on the [product site](https://mixxape.com), or see the [soft-launch news](/news/mixxape-1/).
